The release date is set, and each day brings us closer to that moment when the game will finally land in the hands of those who still cherish old, classic tales—stories where everything is beautiful but just a little bit frightening too.

This isn’t a stroll in the park, no. We’re heading deep into an ancient forest, thick with thorns and tangled roots that don’t much care for your footsteps.

But we have a job to do!

Time will tell!
