Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15/01/2025

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Data:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Gameplay Data: Level progress, in-game actions (e.g., where the player died, time spent on a level), and game preferences.
  • Country: Automatically detected based on your connection.
  • Crash Reports: Data on crashes and errors for improving performance.

Data from Third-Party Services:

  • Unity Analytics: For tracking in-game behavior and performance.
  • Unity Ads: (planned) For displaying in-game advertisements.
  • Unity Cloud Save: For storing game settings and progress (graphics, language preferences).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To improve gameplay experience and balance game mechanics.
  • To analyze user behavior for game development.
  • To provide personalized content and updates.
  • To deliver and manage in-game advertisements (when implemented).
  • To store and synchronize game progress across devices.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

Our Game is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
